Our Work

Creating impact through focused initiatives in livelihood, climate change, and health.

Livelihood Programs

Through the Business Development Project we invest in community savings groups and group businesses for both refugees and host nationals, enabling them to reach their full potential.

Groups receive training in savings management and business skills to maximize profits within their communities.

  • Vocational training in tailoring, carpentry, and more
  • Community Savings Groups
  • Agricultural cooperatives and farming training

Climate Change Interventions:

We address environmental challenges through sustainable practices and community education.

Our climate programs focus on reforestation, clean energy, and waste management to create a greener, more resilient community.

  • Tree planting and conservation programs
  • Solar energy and clean cooking solutions
  • Waste management

Public Health

We improve community health through education, access to care, and preventive measures.

Raising awareness about health issues and improving access to essential health services and information.

  • Humanitarian Response to the DRC Crisis is a project funded by HEKS that supports children and youth with disabilities through medical rehabilitation services at CORSU Hospital.
  • MALARIA Project supports in creating awareness on malaria prevention in Rubondo Zone through working with the health centre and the Village Health Teams who mobilise
    communities and provide Integrated Community Case Management (ICCM).
  • The Multisectoral Integrated Humanitarian Project is an initiative is funded by Action Against Hunger(ACF) and delivers comprehensive support to couples, families, and communities.
